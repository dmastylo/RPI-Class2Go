Help / FAQ

  1. I'm having trouble viewing content on the site!

    If you're having any difficulties viewing content on the site, try using Chrome or Firefox as your browser.

  2. How do I change my password or other personal info?

    If you want to change your password, or any of your profile information, click on your name in the top navigation bar.

  3. How do I watch the videos sped up or slowed down?

    If you disable Flash in your browser, you should get the HTML5 YouTube player when you view a video, and if you click on "Settings" (the cog near the bottom right of your video player), you'll find options to change the speed.

  4. Will I receive Stanford University credit for a Class2Go course?

    No. Class2Go courses do not confer Stanford University credit.

  5. Do I get a statement of accomplishment for completing a course?

    Yes. You will receive a statement of accomplishment for successfully completing a course.

  8. How do I remove myself from course email lists?

    To stop receiving course-related email:

    • Click your name in the upper right corner of your browser.
    • Click Change Info in the lower left corner of your browser.
    • Uncheck Receive emails from courses.
    • Click Save Changes.

  9. Can I drop a class?

    Yes. To drop a class:

    • Click your name in the upper right corner of your browser.
    • Click Unenroll as Student next to the course to drop.
    • Click Save Changes.

  10. Will online students have access to other Stanford University resources and services?

    No. Online students don't have access to other Stanford University services.

  11. Do I have to watch the lectures at a particular time?

    No, you can watch them at a time that's convenient for you.
